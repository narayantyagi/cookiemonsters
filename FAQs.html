Support FAQs


1.Need to return something?

1. Go to My orders.

2. Choose an item that you want to return or replace.

3. Click on return checked item.





2.Want to cancel your order?

1. Go to My orders.

2. Choose an item that you want to cancel and click cancel item.

3. Click on cancel checked item.





3.Track your order.

1. Go to My orders.

2. Go to the order you want to track.

3. Click Track Package next to your order.

4. Select See all Updates to view delivery updates.





4.To Retry a payment decline.

1. Go to My orders.

2. Retry with your payment method by selecting Retry Payment Method next to the order.
